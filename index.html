SES FAQ

SES provides a wealth of knowledge, addressing the most common questions customers ask.
What is a SES telecommunications system?
SES Telecommunication’s systems encompasses wired and wireless local and wide area networks and many technologies providing the capabilities for systems to communicate with each other or with users. SES Telecommunication’s systems involves the exchange of information, such as voice, video and data, using electronic devices.
Which is a SES security system?
An SES security system is a computer system, or network solution that contains some form of access control technology, such as forced authentication, or access control designed to prevent unauthorized persons from entering.
Whom does SES cooperate with?
Smart Engineering Services (SES) has partnerships with major international brands in the field of providing engineering services and consulting, such as AAB, Honeywell, Schneider, Leviton, Access, ICCM, and Siemens.
What is the mission of smart engineering services company?
The mission of the Smart Engineering Services (SES) Company is to provide the highest level of engineering consultancy and smart services, to gain more of the trust of its customers.
Are engineering services important?
Of course, because engineering services integrate scientific and practical principles together, providing systems and processes that in themselves create multiple different paths to project success. The scientific and practical integration of engineering services always helps in innovation and the acquisition of new successful knowledge, this makes engineering services necessary in all aspects of projects.
What is industrial and engineering services?
SES has a proven experience in the field of engineering and consultation services such as electrical studies, asset management of electrical components, electrical shop drawings, etc.
What is SES do?
SES provides smart wide range of solutions for smart buildings such as: Security Systems (Access Control, Biometric Systems, Video Surveillance and Intrusion Detection).
